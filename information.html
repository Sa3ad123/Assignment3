Why We Collect Data on Student Progress at GIC

The purpose of collecting data on student progress at Glasgow International College is to enhance the academic and overall student experience. By monitoring and assessing academic progress, we can identify areas for improvement and implement targeted interventions to support student success.

International Student Support at the University of Glasgow

The University of Glasgow provides comprehensive support for international students, including guidance on visas and immigration, accommodation, and academic matters. The International Student Support team is dedicated to ensuring that all international students have a smooth and enjoyable experience while studying at the University.

FAQs for International Students

For more detailed information on topics such as tuition fees, scholarships, cost of living, job opportunities after graduation, campus visits, and entry requirements, please refer to the International Students FAQ page on the University of Glasgow's website.
